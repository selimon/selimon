The Diwan of Royal Court Welcomes

you to an innovative and challenging initiative designed for you - Oman’s emerging, future generation.

Welcome to a program built to equip young Omanis like you with the attitudes, the skills and the knowledge to be successful in the near future world of work, a confident face and inquisitive mind in the irrepressible 4IR landscape.

Al Nashia Scheme

This is a 2 week intense residential program delivered in Muscat to 150 participants aged between 15-17 years old.

Learn more

Al Shabab Scheme

This is a 2-stage program, Virtual Learning (1000 participants) and Experiential Learning (100 participants) targeting 18-29 years old.
